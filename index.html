My background

"Welcome to my website. I am Ariana Williams, a highly motivated individual with a diverse background and a passion for technology. As a dedicated mother of five children, I successfully juggled the responsibilities of serving in the army while pursuing my educational aspirations. During my military tenure, I completed my bachelor's degree in computer science, demonstrating my commitment to personal and professional growth.
Upon retiring from the army, I embarked on a new chapter by pursuing a master's degree in Software Engineering, further solidifying my expertise in this field. Seeking to expand my knowledge and gain hands-on experience, I sought additional educational opportunities and enrolled in TLG SDE while I was pregnant with my last child.
With a strong academic foundation and a wealth of real-world experience, I am equipped with the skills and determination to excel in the software engineering industry. My journey has shaped my belief in the transformative power of technology, and it is this conviction that drives me to contribute meaningfully to the field.
Join me on this platform as I share my insights, experiences, and expertise in software engineering. Together, let us explore the exciting world of technology and its endless possibilities."
